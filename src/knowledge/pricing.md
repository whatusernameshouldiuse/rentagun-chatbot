# Rentagun Pricing

## Subscription Tiers

### Basic Tier - $99/month
- 1 rental per month
- 7-day rental period
- Firearms up to $1,000 MSRP
- Perfect for: Casual shooters, people comparing options

### Pro Tier - $199/month
- 2 rentals per month
- 7-day rental period each
- Firearms up to $3,000 MSRP
- Perfect for: Enthusiasts, collectors, try-before-you-buy shoppers

## Daily Rental Rates

If you exceed your subscription rentals or rent higher-value items, daily rates apply:

**Formula: 2% of firearm MSRP per day**

### Examples:
| Firearm | MSRP | Daily Rate | 7-Day Total |
|---------|------|------------|-------------|
| Glock 19 | $550 | $11/day | $77 |
| S&W M&P Shield | $450 | $9/day | $63 |
| Desert Eagle .50AE | $1,900 | $38/day | $266 |
| Colt Python | $1,500 | $30/day | $210 |
| HK MP5 .22LR | $500 | $10/day | $70 |

## Additional Fees

### Shipping
- Outbound shipping: Included
- Return shipping: Prepaid label included
- Expedited shipping: +$25

### Security Deposit
A hold (not charge) is placed on your card equal to:
- Basic tier: Up to $1,000
- Pro tier: Up to $3,000
- Released upon successful return inspection

### Damage/Loss
- Minor wear: No charge (expected with use)
- Damage requiring repair: Repair cost charged
- Lost/stolen firearm: Full MSRP charged
- Accessories lost (mag, case): Replacement cost

## Rental to Purchase

**Love what you rented? Keep it!**

If you decide to purchase a firearm you've rented:
- Rental fees apply toward purchase price
- Complete purchase through your FFL
- Standard FFL transfer fees apply

Example: Rent a Glock 19 for $77 (7 days), decide to buy for $550.
Your cost: $550 - $77 rental credit = $473 + FFL transfer fee

## Payment

- Credit/debit card required
- Card authorized at booking
- Charged upon shipment
- Security deposit released 5-7 business days after return inspection
